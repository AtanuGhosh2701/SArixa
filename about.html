About Us

Hi there 👋

We’re really glad you’re here.

SArixa is a simple, free tool that helps you turn images into PDFs—right inside your browser. No sign-ups, no hidden costs, no unnecessary steps. Just open it, use it, and you’re done.

⚠️ The Problem

If you’ve ever used online PDF tools, you probably know the drill.

  • ❌ You upload your files.
  • ❌ You wait.
  • ❌ And you’re left wondering where those files actually go.

And sometimes, it just feels… uncomfortable.

Your images might be personal. Notes, documents, IDs—things you don’t want floating around on some unknown server. On top of that, many tools are slow, full of ads, or suddenly ask you to pay.

That never felt right to us.

💡 The SArixa Solution

So we built SArixa differently.

Your files never leave your device. Everything happens right inside your browser. That means:

  • ✅ No uploads
  • ✅ No servers
  • ✅ No tracking your files

It’s just your device doing the work.

Because of that, it’s also fast. Even on low-end phones, it runs smoothly. No heavy loading screens. No waiting for files to travel back and forth.

🛡️ What We Stand For

We kept three things in mind while building SArixa:

🆓 Free

You shouldn’t have to pay for basic tools like this. Ever.

🔒 Private

Your files are yours. Period.

⚡ Fast

Tools should save time, not waste it.

Nothing complicated. Just useful things that actually help.

Thanks for being here. It really means a lot.

About Us

Hi there 👋

We’re really glad you’re here.

SArixa is a simple, free tool that helps you turn images into PDFs—right inside your browser. No sign-ups, no hidden costs, no unnecessary steps.

⚠️ The Problem

  • ❌ You upload your files.
  • ❌ You wait.
  • ❌ And you’re left wondering where those files actually go.

Your images might be personal. Notes, documents, IDs—things you don’t want floating around on some unknown server. That never felt right to us.

💡 The SArixa Solution

Your files never leave your device. Everything happens right inside your browser. That means:

  • ✅ No uploads
  • ✅ No servers
  • ✅ No tracking

🔒 Private

Your files are yours. Period.

⚡ Fast

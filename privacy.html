Hi, We Care About Your Privacy

At SArixa, We believe that your photos and documents are your own business. That’s why I built this tool to be as safe as possible.

"We don’t store your images. Period. Everything happens right inside your browser."

How SArixa Works

When you use our Image-to-PDF tool, your files never touch our servers. All the processing is done by your own device's browser. This is the safest way to convert files online.

The Information We See

We don't have accounts or logins. The only time we see your details is if you voluntarily use our Contact Form to say 'Hi' or report a bug. In that case, we only use your email to reply to you.

Ads and Analytics

To keep SArixa free, we show ads through Google AdSense. Google uses 'cookies' to show you relevant ads. You can always turn this off in your browser settings if you're not comfortable.

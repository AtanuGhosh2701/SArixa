Frequently Asked Questions

Are my photos really safe? Do you upload them anywhere?

Yes, they are 100% safe! We never upload your photos to any server. The whole PDF conversion happens right inside your own browser. Your files stay on your device the entire time.

Is this actually free? Will you add a watermark to my PDF?

It's completely free, forever. No hidden fees, no required sign-ups, and absolutely no watermarks on your exported PDFs.

Can I combine a bunch of images into one single PDF?

You sure can! Just select or drag-and-drop all the images you want, drag them to arrange the order, and hit generate. It'll compile them all into one neat PDF file for you.

What if my image files are really big?

Since the processing uses your own device and not our servers, there aren't any strict size limits. Just keep in mind that if you add dozens of massive photos at once, it might take your device a few extra seconds to process them.

Does this work on my phone too?

Absolutely! It works perfectly on Androids, iPhones, and tablets. You can even use the built-in "Camera" button to snap pictures of your notes and turn them straight into a PDF.

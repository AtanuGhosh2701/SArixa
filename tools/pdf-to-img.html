What is a PDF to Image Converter?

A PDF to Image converter is a smart tool that reads a multi-page PDF document and transforms each individual page into a standalone image file, like a JPG or PNG. This makes it incredibly easy to share specific pages on social media, embed them in presentations, or edit them in standard image software.
